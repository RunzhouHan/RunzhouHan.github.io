I'm a second year ECE Ph.D. student at Iowa State University. I'm a member of the Data Storage Lab (DSL) maintained by Dr. Mai Zheng. Before I came to ISU, I recieved an MS degree in ECE from Boston University and a BS degree in physics from Wuhan Univeristy. Here is my CV.

My research interest primarily focuses on the reliability issues in HPC storage systems, especially File System Checkers (fsck). My current research projects are related to applying automated analysis techniques to parallel file systems, file system log analysis and serverless computing.
